Scam awareness
Do not lose your money to scams

Scammers in the UAE target workers. These are the most common ones. If you see any of these — stop, and contact me.

⚠️
"Double your money" promisesNo real investment doubles your money fast. Anyone who says this is lying. Walk away.
⚠️
Someone asks for your passport or Emirates IDOnly banks and government offices need your original documents. No investment advisor does.
⚠️
WhatsApp investment groupsScammers build trust slowly in groups, then ask for money. Real investments are never sold this way.
⚠️
Pay money to receive moneyAny scheme asking you to pay first to get paid later is always a scam. Always.
⚠️
Job offers that seem too goodHigh pay, easy work, and an upfront payment asked of you — it is a scam.
